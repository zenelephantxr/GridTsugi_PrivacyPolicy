## Summary (TL;DR)

- The Game is designed to work **offline** and **does not require an internet connection** for core gameplay.
- We store **gameplay data locally on your device** (e.g., scores, levels completed, time taken, and a spatial-anchor room ID to keep your room setup).
- When you submit a leaderboard entry, your **PICO User ID, display name (alias), profile picture URL, and score** are sent to PICO's Platform servers via the PICO Platform SDK.
- We **do not** use third-party analytics or ad SDKs and do **not** sell or share your data with advertisers.
- GridTsugi: Maze of Woven Bridges includes an online leaderboard feature that allows players to compare their level completion times with other players globally and among friends, powered by the PICO Platform.

---

## Information We Process

### A. Gameplay & Progress Data (local)

- **Scores and performance metrics** (e.g., score value, time taken to complete a level)
- **Progress tracking** (e.g., played levels, won levels, total number of levels completed)
- **Completion stats** (e.g., best times, streaks, attempts)

We use this data solely to run core game features (continue your progress, show stats/achievements, on-device difficulty tuning).

### B. PICO Platform User Data (for online leaderboards)

When online leaderboards are active, we collect and process data from the PICO Platform SDK including User ID (PICO ID), basic user profile information (display name / alias, profile picture URL), and friends list data (limited to friends who also play the Game). See the "Online Leaderboard Feature" section for complete details.

### C. Spatial & Room Setup Data (local)

- **Spatial anchor room ID** (or similar anchor/scene identifier) used to remember where you scanned/placed the grid in your room so you don't have to redo setup each time.

We do **not** store passthrough video frames or raw room scans ourselves. Any room-mapping, tracking, or sensor processing is handled by the PICO system. We only keep a local identifier that lets the Game relocate your previously placed content.

### D. Optional Contact Data You Provide

- If you **manually contact us** (e.g., via email or form) you might provide **your name, email address, or phone number**.
- This is **outside the Game** and is used only to respond to your inquiry/support request. It is **not required** to play.

## What We Do Not Collect or Use

- No facial tracking, camera frames, or raw depth/mesh data saved by us.
- No advertising SDKs or third-party analytics.
- No biometric or eye-tracking data is read or stored by the Game.
- No sale or "sharing" of personal data (as defined by many privacy laws).

## Where Your Data Lives & Transfers

- **On-device only (by default):** Local Game data listed above is saved in the Game's private storage on your PICO device and is **not transmitted to us**.
- **Platform backups:** If you enable **PICO cloud backup** at the OS level, your device may back up the Game's local data to PICO's cloud. That backup is a platform feature controlled by PICO and your device settings — we do not control or receive that backup.
- **PICO Platform data (leaderboards):** User ID, alias, profile picture URL, friends list, and leaderboard scores are transmitted to and stored on PICO's Platform servers. This data is subject to PICO's Privacy Policy and international data transfer safeguards.
  * Non-Chinese-Mainland accounts: routed through PICO's global infrastructure (e.g., `platform-us.picovr.com` and related regional endpoints).
  * Chinese-Mainland accounts: routed through PICO's domestic infrastructure operated by Beijing PICO Technology Co., Ltd. and subject to PRC data laws.

## Security

- Data is stored in the Game's **private app storage**. We rely on the device's operating system sandboxing and platform security.

- We do not add extra encryption beyond what the OS and PICO SDK provide. Because all local data remains on-device (unless you enable OS backups), risk of third-party access is reduced.

## Online Leaderboard Feature

GridTsugi: Maze of Woven Bridges includes an online leaderboard feature that allows players to compare their level completion times with other players globally and among friends, powered by the PICO Platform SDK.

### What Data We Collect for Leaderboards

When you play the Game while signed in to a PICO account, we collect and process the following data through the PICO Platform SDK to provide leaderboard functionality:

- **User ID (PICO ID / OpenID):** A unique identifier assigned by PICO to your account. We use this to associate your scores with your account and identify your position on leaderboards.
- **User Profile Information:** Your PICO display name (alias) and profile picture URL (`ImageUrl`). We display this information on leaderboards so you and other players can see recognizable player identities instead of anonymous IDs.
- **Friends List:** Information about your PICO friends who also play the Game . We use this to provide friend-specific leaderboard views and highlight your friends' positions on global leaderboards. We only see friends who have also installed the Game and granted the relevant permission.
- **Gameplay Performance Data:** Your level completion times, scores, optional supplementary tiebreaker metrics, and level/leaderboard API name identifiers. This data is submitted to and stored on PICO's leaderboard servers to calculate and display rankings.

### How We Use This Data

We use the data collected above solely for the following purposes:

- **Submit and retrieve leaderboard entries** — Recording your completion times and displaying rankings.
- **Personalize leaderboard displays** — Highlighting "Your Position" or "Your Rank" on leaderboards.
- **Display player identities** — Showing aliases and profile pictures next to leaderboard entries for recognition.
- **Enable friend-based features** — Filtering leaderboards to show only your friends' scores and highlighting friends on global leaderboards.
- **Maintain competitive integrity** — Associating scores with specific user accounts to prevent duplicate or fraudulent entries.

We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, selling to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to providing the leaderboard functionality.

### Where Leaderboard Data Is Stored

- Leaderboard data (User ID, scores, times, aliases, profile picture URLs) is transmitted to and stored on PICO's Platform servers via the PICO Platform SDK. We **do not operate our own separate leaderboard servers**.
- For accounts based outside Chinese Mainland, requests are routed to PICO's global infrastructure (e.g., `platform-us.picovr.com`). For accounts based in Chinese Mainland, requests are routed to PICO's domestic infrastructure. The SDK automatically selects the correct region based on the logged-in account.
- This data may be transferred internationally as part of PICO's global infrastructure, subject to PICO's data protection safeguards.

### Data Retention for Leaderboards

- **Active leaderboard entries:** Retained on PICO's servers as long as they remain within the configured leaderboard or until you request deletion.
- **User profile data:** Cached temporarily for display purposes and refreshed periodically from PICO's servers.
- **Historical data:** May be retained by PICO's Platform services according to their retention policies.

### Your Rights And Choices

You have the following rights regarding your leaderboard data:

- **Opt out:** You can choose not to participate in leaderboards by playing offline or by not signing in to your PICO account when launching the Game. Local progress will still work.
- **Request deletion:** You can request deletion of your leaderboard entries by contacting us at the email below. We will work with PICO to remove your data from leaderboards within 30 days.
- **View your data:** You can see your leaderboard entries within the Game's leaderboard interface.
- **Control profile visibility:** Your alias and profile picture visibility are controlled through your PICO account settings.

### Legal Basis (EU/EEA users)

- **Consent:** By playing levels while signed in and having your scores submitted to leaderboards, you consent to the collection and processing of your leaderboard data as described.
- **Legitimate interests:** Providing competitive social features enhances gameplay experience and player engagement.
